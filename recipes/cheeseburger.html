Cheeseburgers are an american favorite, what's not to like. Beef, cheese, bread and whatever you can think of to add to it. Here's a quick guide in case you've never made these.

  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 8 Buns
  • 16 Slices American Cheese
  1. Shape ground beef into 4 patties
  2. Grill patties until 175 degrees internal
  3. Add burgers to buns and add cheese on top
