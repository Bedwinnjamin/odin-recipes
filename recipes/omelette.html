I eat at least 8 eggs a week, it might be unhealthy, but holy cow. Eggs are cheap, super easy to cook, tasty, and can be turned into many different meals. Omelettes are easy and simple, here is a quick guide on how to make one.

  • 2-3 Eggs
  • Choice of Cheese
  • Fixins
  1. Heat pan
  2. scramble eggs in bowl while pan heats
  3. Add eggs
  4. Flip egg patty after it loses liquidity
  5. Add cheese/fixins, fold, serve
