Lasagna is an interesting dish of lasagna noodles and tomato sauce with many different fixins to put in. Mainly fetta cheese as a base, you can use these ingredients as a guide or just a suggestion

  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 2 Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • 18oz of Fetta Cheese
  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Layer noodles between sauce and fixins
  3. Bake for 30 minutes in 400 degree oven
